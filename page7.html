FM Mohamed bin Zayed receives Qatari

Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Force`s, today received visiting Sheikh Mohammed bin Abdulrahman Al-Thani, Qatari Minister of Foreign Affairs, at Al Shati Palace. His Highness Sheikh Mohamed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Force`s, today received visiting Sheikh Mohammed bin Abdulrahman Al-Thani, Qatari Minister of Foreign Affairs, at Al Shati Palace.

17 February 2016

2015

Abu Dhabi Education Council - September 2005.

Included goals that established the Council for which the first two main goals the development of education and educational institutions in the Emirate of Abu Dhabi , and the second objective to provide technical advice on policies and educational services in the emirate's development , according to a vision aimed at improving education in the Emirate of Abu Dhabi to the highest international standards .
